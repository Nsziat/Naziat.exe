Inside Dhaka City

Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, is a bustling city with a rich history. It serves as the political, economic, and cultural center of the country. The city features a blend of modern skyscrapers and historic landmarks. Notable attractions include Lalbagh Fort and Ahsan Manzil. Despite challenges like traffic and pollution, Dhaka retains its unique charm.

Negative Points:

  1. Dhaka experiences severe traffic jams, making commuting time-consuming
  2. The city faces high levels of air and water pollution due to industrial activities
  3. Dhaka struggles with overcrowding
  4. Many areas of the city suffer from inadequate infrastructure
  5. Dhaka is prone to flooding during the monsoon season, exacerbated by inadequate drainage systems
